How TinyHaus was born

I'm Vlad, and I was an early employee at a media company with millions of newsletter subscribers as a PM. Within weeks, I was drowning in data requests. Editorial wanted engagement metrics. The business team needed subscriber analytics. Marketing couldn't track campaign performance. Everyone was exporting CSVs manually and piecing together reports in Google Sheets.


It was chaos. We were spending hours every week on ad-hoc requests, decisions were delayed, and nothing was reliable.


So I built the solution myself. API integrations to pull data from our email platform, CRM, and content systems. ETL pipelines to process millions of subscriber actions daily. A data warehouse to centralize everything. Dashboards that updated automatically.


Then it grew. Internal tools for the editorial team. Third-party integrations with ad platforms. Events data tracking. The system scaled from newsletter analytics to powering decisions across the entire company.


The impact was massive. Leadership could see real-time performance. We stopped wasting engineering hours on one-off reports. And we did it without hiring a full data team or waiting months for consultants.


That's when I realized: so many growing companies hit this same wall. TinyHaus builds exactly what you need - no over-engineering, no missing pieces. Just clean data infrastructure that works.
